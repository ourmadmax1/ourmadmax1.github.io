Introduction to Astrology

Astrology is the study of the movements and relative positions of celestial bodies as a means for divining information about human affairs and terrestrial events. It has been practiced for centuries in various forms around the world.

Understanding Horoscopes

Horoscopes are astrological charts or predictions based on the positions of celestial bodies at a particular time, such as a person's birthdate. They are often used to provide insights into an individual's personality, relationships, and future events.
